Bracelets

    In these periods, it is thought that bracelets were used in religious ceremonies and rituals rather than being ornamental items that are used today. Because it is seen that the first bracelets were made of sea shells, bones, and ostentatious small stones rather than being a single piece. The purpose of making these pieces into bracelets is to be used in rituals or ceremonies. It is even thought that the sounds it makes make the ceremony more mysterious. Civilization has produced bracelets from different materials day by day. However, none of them took the place of gold bracelets because the material produced was more expensive than the others. Main materials such as bronze, silver, glass and quartz are also frequently used in bracelet production. However, BC. Beginning from the 4th century, handwork on bracelets began to gain importance. Today, for the Turkish people, it is one of the indispensable weddings. It shows that in many societies in Anatolian civilizations, the bracelet was used not only by women but also by men. Optionally, the material of the bracelet is selected and if there is a stone in the design, it is added from the options of diamond, emerald or ruby. Depending on the options, the price of the bracelet also changes.

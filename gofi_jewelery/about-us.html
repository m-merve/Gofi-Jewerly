About Us

   We are working to provide a high quality service with our talented designers, support team, style consultants, master production team in order to realize the wishes of our valued guests and make their dreams come true. There are two production options: to produce your design and to produce the custom made design for you.

While producing your designs;
    You can contact our support team when you want your products with sentimental value to be reproduced. If you want to make improvements on the design, you will be directed to our style consultants by the support team and rearrangement will be made. When no changes are requested, it is directed to our design team and the designs are renewed.

While producing special designs for you;
    When you want to have special designs for yourself or your loved ones, contact our support team and our designers will prepare special designs that only you can have. The designs will be shaped according to your wishes.

After the design phase is completed;
    Before the designs that are approved by you are produced, material options are presented to you. In the materials to be used; Gold, silver, steel, copper and rope options are available. If there is a stone in the design, the materials selected from the diamond, ruby, bead, emerald and natural stone options are transferred to the production team by our design team. Pricing for each material is different. Afterwards, production is made with the material and design you have chosen.


Our Mission

    Gofi Jewelry; produces its jewelry with a combination of personalized designs and local, quality materials. Our guests decide on the product materials, and pricing is made according to the selected materials. Approved products are produced in a short time and delivered to their owners by fast delivery method.


Our Vision

    Gofijewelry.com, which has developed very successful works with unique designs and unique products; aims to develop, rise and become a world-renowned brand. We aim to increase the number of employees over time, to increase the workforce, and to progress by focusing especially on women's employment. It was established online in 2020 and we aim to reach everyone by establishing a chain of stores.
